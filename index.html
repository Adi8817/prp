What is PRP Therapy?

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy is a natural and non-surgical treatment that uses your own blood to promote healing and regeneration. It is widely used for hair regrowth, skin rejuvenation, and joint pain relief. The treatment involves extracting platelets from your blood and injecting them into targeted areas to accelerate repair and restore youthfulness.
