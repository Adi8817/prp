About Our PRP Clinic

What is PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) Therapy?

PRP is a natural, non-surgical treatment that uses the healing properties of your own blood. A small sample of your blood is taken, processed to separate the platelets, and then re-injected into targeted areas to stimulate tissue repair, collagen production, and regeneration. It’s a popular treatment in both skincare and haircare.

PRP For Hair Loss

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy is a non-surgical, regenerative treatment designed to stimulate hair growth by using the body’s own healing abilities. The procedure involves drawing a small amount of the patient’s blood, processing it to extract concentrated platelets rich in growth factors and bioactive proteins, and injecting the plasma directly into the scalp. These natural elements work to rejuvenate hair follicles, promote new growth, and slow down hair thinning.

PRP is derived from your own blood, making it a safe and biocompatible solution. The concentrated platelets contain powerful growth factors that assist in cell repair and tissue regeneration. When injected into areas of thinning hair, PRP enhances blood supply to the follicles and increases the thickness of the hair shaft.

PRP is especially beneficial for individuals experiencing androgenetic alopecia (male or female pattern baldness). While clinical research is still ongoing, many patients have reported noticeable improvements in hair density and scalp coverage.

At a Glance

  • Recommended Frequency: One session per month for 3 months Maintenance sessions every 3–4 months.
  • Procedure Time: Approximately 60 minutes.
  • Side Effects: Mild redness, tenderness, or swelling at the injection site.

PRP Treatment for Skin Rejuvenation

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy, also known as the “Vampire Facial,” is a cutting-edge aesthetic treatment that harnesses the body’s own healing power to restore a youthful glow to the skin. This natural procedure involves drawing a small amount of your own blood, isolating the platelet-rich plasma packed with growth factors, and gently injecting or micro-needling it into targeted areas of the face to stimulate collagen production and tissue regeneration.

PRP is rich in essential growth factors that trigger the skin’s natural repair process. Once injected or applied via micro-needling, it promotes the regeneration of skin cells, boosts collagen levels, and enhances overall skin tone and texture—leaving the skin looking fresher, firmer, and more radiant.

PRP is especially beneficial for individuals experiencing androgenetic alopecia (male or female pattern baldness). While clinical research is still ongoing, many patients have reported noticeable improvements in hair density and scalp coverage.

At a Glance

  • Recommended Frequency: One session every 4 weeks for optimal results Maintenance every 4–6 months.
  • Procedure Time: 45–60 minutes.
  • Side Effects: Mild redness, swelling, or sensitivity for 1–2 days.
